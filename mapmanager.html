TrialsManager Client

TrialsManager is a portable, simple tool to manage custom maps/tracks for Trial games. With the official servers being unpredictable, we wanted a way for people to be able to download, upload, import, export, and overall just manage tracks easily. However, if you wish to download maps from this site without the program, we offer a tutorial here.

(We hate programs that clutter up systems too; we won't leave any extra files on your pc!)
