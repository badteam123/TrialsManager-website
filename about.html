About

Trials Map Manager is an alternative way to share and play maps on Trials Games.

Unfortunately, Ubisoft has been providing minimal support to keep the Trial's servers running.
That's why this exists. To provide an alternative way to download, upload, and manage maps.

Help

Sharing [&] Playing Tracks

Playing Maps/Tracks is easy with the Trials Map Manager program, but it's perfectly fine if you don't want to use it.

You require the program to upload maps, but not for downloading.
